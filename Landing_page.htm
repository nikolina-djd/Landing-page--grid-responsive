  • Campanula flower

    Campanula is a group of plant species that have bell-shaped flowers. They come in a variety of colors, including white, blue, lavender, pink and rose. Some species reach only 6 inches (15 cm) high, while others reach heights ranging from 4 feet to 6 feet (1 m to 2 m)

  • Cowslip flower

    Like many other spring flowers, the Cowslip is closely associated with English folklore and tradition, including adorning garlands for May Day and being strewn on church paths for weddings. The Cowslip displays a rosette of green, crinkly, tongue-like leaves, low to the ground.
